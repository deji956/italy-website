Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking popular attractions and tours 2-3 months in advance, especially for peak season (April-October). Last-minute bookings are sometimes available but not guaranteed.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled visit for most attractions. Some experiences require 48-72 hours notice. Full details provided at booking.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 10+ people qualify for special pricing on most tours and attractions. Contact us for a custom quote.

Are your guides licensed?

Absolutely. All our tour guides are licensed, English-speaking professionals with extensive knowledge of Italian history and culture.

Can you create custom itineraries?

Yes! We specialize in personalized travel experiences. Tell us your interests, budget, and travel dates, and we'll design the perfect Italian adventure for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for group bookings.
